This ontology covers the assets and condition assesment of a sewage system, mirroring the elements as defined per the national advisory leaflet DWA-M 150 of German sewage systems. https://shop.dwa.de/DWA-M-150-Datenaustauschformat-fuer-die-Zustandserfassung-von-Entwaesserungssystemen-April-2010-Stand-korrigierte-Fassung-November-2018/M-150-10 This ontology was created as part of a Bachelors Thesis into an investigation of existing ontologies for underground infrastructure.
