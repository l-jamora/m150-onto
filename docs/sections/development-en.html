Ontology Development back to ToC

The development of M150-Onto follows a methodological approach that combines several established ontology development strategies. As described by Zeb (2020) in their development of the Cats_Onto ontology, this approach synthesizes methodologies from Uschold and Gruninger (1996), Fernandez, Gomez-Pearez, and Juristo (1997), and Noy and McGuiness (2001).
